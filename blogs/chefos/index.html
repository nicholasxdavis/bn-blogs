Introducing ChefOS

Nov 6, 2025 by Blacnova

Today, we’re introducing ChefOS, the complete kitchen management toolkit. Designed specifically for professional chefs, caterers, and kitchen managers, this comprehensive application streamlines every aspect of kitchen operations—from recipe development and scaling to menu costing and inventory management—all from a single, unified platform.

A Centralized & Scalable Recipe Hub

ChefOS consolidates your entire recipe library into one centralized, searchable location. But the core of the platform is its powerful Recipe Scaler. You can scale any recipe from 1 portion to 1,000. Our professional scaling engine handles weights, volumes, and units flawlessly, saving you hours of manual calculation and preventing costly errors.

Streamlined Menu & Cost Control

Build, manage, and cost your menus with ease. You can combine your saved, scaled recipes into complete service menus, plan daily specials, and attach PDF files, all in one organized hub. From there, you can instantly generate Shopping Lists from any menu or recipe. This allows you to manage suppliers, track prices, and calculate subtotals to keep your food costs in check.

The Complete Kitchen Toolkit

Alongside the core management tools, we’re introducing a suite of essential utilities built for a professional kitchen. This includes a powerful Kitchen Calculator for instant unit conversions (grams to oz, cups to tbsp) and ingredient density calculations. It also features a Chef Calendar for planning operations and PDF Management to keep all your important documents linked and organized.

Your Data, Your Way

We believe your data is yours. ChefOS gives you the choice. You can store all your recipes and data locally on your device for complete privacy and offline access, or you can use the optional ChefOS Cloud Sync to access your kitchen from any device, anywhere.
