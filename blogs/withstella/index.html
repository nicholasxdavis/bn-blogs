Introducing Withstella

Nov 6, 2025 by Blacnova

Withstella isn’t just another cloud service like Dropbox, Google Drive, or iCloud. It’s built specifically for teams, designers, and businesses. Stella is a collaborative cloud platform designed to streamline teamwork and asset management.

With Stella, you can create fully customizable, manageable, and shareable Kits. Think of Kits as advanced shared folders—perfect for organizing projects that involve multiple designers, editors, or writers. Each Kit serves as a central hub for all files and assets related to a specific project, business, or brand.

Stella is built to adapt to a wide range of workflows, making it a versatile solution for any operation.

A Central Hub for Your Brand

Withstella consolidates your entire brand identity into one place with Stella Kits. Instead of juggling style guides in PDFs and logos in scattered folders, you get a centralized, manageable hub for all essential assets. This includes complete logo management, color palette definitions, typography guidelines, and version control for all brand assets, ensuring your team always has the correct version.

The Single Source of Truth for Assets

The Asset Hub is your project's single source of truth. Think of it as a powerful, intelligent library for all your brand assets—not just file storage. We've built-in real-time version control, smart tagging, and granular access permissions. You can effortlessly organize, search, and distribute images, documents, fonts, and video, all from one secure, centralized library.

Collaboration Built for Teams

Alongside the Asset Hub, we're introducing Team Spaces. This feature allows you to collaborate securely with dedicated workspaces for your team. You can set granular, role-based permissions (Owner, Admin, Editor, Viewer) to control exactly who can view, edit, and approve brand assets. It's the perfect way to manage approval workflows and keep all your project stakeholders in sync.

Actionable Brand Insights

The first thing you'll notice when you use Withstella is the clarity it provides. Our Analytics Dashboard gives you an instant, detailed overview of your brand's performance. It tracks asset downloads, content usage, and team activity, providing valuable insights into how, where, and by whom your brand assets are being used.
